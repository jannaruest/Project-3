About

You're on a farm, it's harvest season and you need to make sure you have all the fruit for the winter!

Goal: Catch 10 ripe Apples in the basket & avoid the rotten fruit! Or else you'll be hungry for the winter!

RULES

  • Use either A & D keys, or Left & Right Arrow keys to move

  • Catch ripe fruit: +1
  • Avoid rotten fruit: -2

  • Goal: 10 points
