Harvest Season!

It's harvest season on the apple farm, and you need to make sure you have enough fruit for the winter!

Rules:

Catch at least 20 ripe Apples in the basket or else you'll be hungry for the winter!

(Avoid any rotten apples as well as other fruits that may fall! Too bad for the inconsiderate neighbours, you're an apple farm not a grocery store.)

Points:

  • Apples: +1
  • Rotten Apples: -2
  • Non-Apple fruit: -3

Controls:

Move using "A" and "D" keys, or ⬅ & ➡ keys
